### Slide 2: The Problem
*   **Manual Intake:** Bulk gifting enquiries are scattered across emails and spreadsheets.
*   **Workflow Bottlenecks:** Coordinating between sales, designers, and production is slow.
*   **Lack of Tracking:** Clients have no visibility into design approvals or dispatch status.
*   **Slow Quoting:** Generating custom quotes for personalized hampers takes days.

---

### Slide 3: The Solution (Our Portal)
*   **Unified Intake:** A single, smart portal for clients to submit detailed bulk enquiries.
*   **Role-Based Dashboards:** Dedicated views for Sales, Admin, Designers, Production, and Dispatch teams.
*   **AI-Powered Recommendations:** Smart suggestions for packages and automated follow-up messages.
*   **End-to-End Tracking:** Kanban boards and workflow tracking from submission to delivery.

---

### Slide 4: Key Features (Step 1 - Interfaces)
*   **Corporate Enquiry Portal:** Seamless submission form.
*   **Product Personalization Manager:** Manage bulk engravings and greeting cards.
*   **Design Approval Tracker:** Visual kanban board for artwork sign-offs.
*   **Occasion Calendar:** Track client milestones and festivals.
*   **Inventory & Returns:** Stock management and RMA tracking.

---

### Slide 5: System Architecture (Steps 2-4)
*   **Frontend:** React (Vite) with dynamic routing and responsive dashboards.
*   **Backend:** Python Flask APIs with MySQL database.
*   **AI Integration:** Google Gemini API for intelligent package recommendations and quotation summaries.
*   **Workflow Engine:** Rule-based state machine for moving enquiries through stages.

---

### Slide 6: The AI Advantage
*   **Contextual Packages:** AI analyzes budget, category, and quantity to suggest the best kits.
*   **Automated Drafting:** Gemini drafts professional email follow-ups based on the current enquiry state.
*   **Efficiency:** Reduces manual quoting time by 80%.

### Slide 7: Next Steps & Future Roadmap
*   **Integration:** Connect with external shipping providers (FedEx, DHL).
*   **Payment Gateway:** Enable bulk invoice payments directly through the portal.
*   **Advanced Analytics:** Predictive stock reordering based on upcoming occasions.
