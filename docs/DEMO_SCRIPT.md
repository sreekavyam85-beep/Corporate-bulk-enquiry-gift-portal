# Demo Video Script: Corporate Bulk Gift Enquiry Portal

**Duration:** ~3 Minutes
**Required Setup:** Have the frontend and backend running locally. Open the application in your browser.

---

### Scene 1: The Client Enquiry (0:00 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Show the main `EnquiryForm` page.
**Voiceover:** "Welcome to the Paper Plane Corporate Bulk Gifting Portal. Let's see how a corporate buyer submits a new request."
**Action:** 
1. Fill out the form with sample data (Company: Acme Corp, Category: Festival Hampers, Quantity: 500, Budget: 50).
2. Check 'Branding Required' and add a note.
3. Click "Submit Enquiry".
**Voiceover:** "The backend APIs instantly validate and save this data, logging the timestamp and assigning an initial status."

---

### Scene 2: The Sales Dashboard & Workflow (0:45 - 1:30)
**Visual:** Navigate to the `Sales Dashboard`.
**Voiceover:** "Now, let's switch roles to the Corporate Sales Team. Here in the Sales Dashboard, we see our KPI metrics update in real-time."
**Action:**
1. Point out the KPI cards.
2. Click on the newly submitted enquiry in the table to open the `Workflow Screen`.
**Voiceover:** "Opening the enquiry takes us to the Workflow tracking board, where the system applies our corporate gifting pipeline."

---

### Scene 3: AI Recommendations (1:30 - 2:00)
**Visual:** Highlight the `RecommendationPanel` on the Workflow Screen.
**Voiceover:** "To speed up the quoting process, our integrated AI Assistant analyzes the buyer's budget and requirements to suggest the perfect package."
**Action:**
1. Click the "Generate AI Recommendations" button.
2. Show the generated package summary and the auto-drafted follow-up message.

---

### Scene 4: Advanced Role Dashboards (2:00 - 2:45)
**Visual:** Use the Sidebar Navigation to click through the different Role Views.
**Voiceover:** "Our portal isn't just for sales. We have dedicated, role-specific dashboards to manage the entire lifecycle."
**Action:**
1. Click **Designer Dashboard**: "Designers can track artwork approvals."
2. Click **Production Dashboard**: "The production team manages assembly and inventory."
3. Click **Dispatch Dashboard**: "And logistics handles shipping schedules."
4. Click **Advanced Apps** (Personalization, Calendar, Returns): Briefly show these screens exist to handle edge cases.

---

### Scene 5: Conclusion (2:45 - 3:00)
**Visual:** Return to the Sales Dashboard or Homepage.
**Voiceover:** "From initial bulk enquiry to final dispatch, this smart prototype automates and organizes the entire B2B gifting workflow. Thank you for watching."
